📘 How to Use This Tracker

  • 💰 Set your monthly budget in the Overview tab.
  • 🧾 Add expenses with the “Add Expense” button or Quick Add options.
  • 📊 Use the Analytics and Trends tabs to understand your spending patterns.
  • 🕹️ Switch between currencies: £, $, €, ¥ in the Quick Actions panel.
  • 💳 Choose your payment method: Credit/Debit Card, Bank Transfer, or Direct Debit.
  • 🌗 Toggle Dark Mode via the ⚙️ Settings menu.
  • 📤 Import and 📥 Export your data through the Settings menu.
  • 📝 Export a Monthly Summary with detailed stats and top 3 categories.
  • 📱 Tip: On mobile, tap the tab buttons to switch views.

🔁 Don’t forget to export your data regularly to avoid losing it if your browser is cleared.
